Free Wifi in Wellington

Free Wifi offered by City Council and Spark Mobile

CBDfree

CBD Free is the free wifi throughout Wellington city. It is straight forward to use and has a pretty good range of service.

Spark Free Wifi

If your mobille phone is with Spark, you can use up to 1GB free data for free near these phone booths in the City.

Mobile Vehicles

Mobile rental vehicles in Wellington city

Mobile Scooters

There are currently 2 mobile scooters in Wellington, Jump by Uber and Flamingo. In order to find and rent these electric scooters, you need to install their apps on your phone and find them on the map. Once you've found them, you can unlock the vehicle via app by scanning the QR code. They charge for the vehicle in-app as well.

Onzo Bike

These bicyles works same as the scooters.

Transportation Live

These are some examples of the live transportation status in Wellington.

Time to Destination

This is the sign you can find near the enterance of the highway. It tells you approximately how long it would take from Wellington to nearby suburb, updated time to time.

Speed Meter

This is a speed meter on Manners Street telling you how fast you are going on the street.

Bus Sign

These bus signs around the city gets updated frequently telling passengers how long it would take for the buses to arrive.
